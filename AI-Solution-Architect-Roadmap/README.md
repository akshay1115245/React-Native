# Applied AI Solution Architect — Zero to Expert Roadmap

> **Audience:** Principal Mobile Engineer (10+ years) with zero AI/ML background  
> **Goal:** Become a credible, hands-on Applied AI Solution Architect  
> **Approach:** Depth-first, practical, project-driven — leveraging your existing engineering maturity

---

## Why This Roadmap Is Different for You

You are not starting from scratch as an engineer. You have:
- Deep systems thinking from 10+ years of production software
- Architecture patterns, API design, distributed systems intuition
- Code quality discipline and debugging expertise
- Understanding of product delivery and stakeholder communication

This roadmap **accelerates you through fundamentals** where your engineering experience overlaps, and **slows down deliberately** where AI/ML introduces genuinely new mental models (probability, linear algebra applied to learning, LLM reasoning chains, etc.).

**Estimated commitment:** 10–15 hours/week of focused study + practice

---

## How to Use This Roadmap

1. **Work sequentially through phases** — each builds on the previous
2. **Do every practical exercise** — reading without coding teaches nothing in AI
3. **Build the capstone project** for each phase before moving on
4. **Connect concepts back to mobile** — this makes you unique: an architect who understands client-side AI, on-device inference, mobile-first AI UX
5. **Document your learning** — maintain an `AI-Journal.md` to track decisions, confusion, and insights

## Your Unique Positioning as a Mobile + AI Architect

Your 10.6 years of mobile engineering is a **competitive moat**, not a disadvantage. Target these high-value intersections:

- **On-device AI** (CoreML, TensorFlow Lite, MediaPipe) — most AI architects don't know this domain
- **AI-native mobile app architecture** — LLM integration patterns in mobile clients
- **Edge AI systems design** — federated learning, on-device inference pipelines
- **Mobile telemetry for ML** — using mobile analytics data as training signal
- **AI-powered developer tooling** — code generation, test automation, crash analysis
